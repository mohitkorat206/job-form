Note : How to generate App Password Key

For Gmail Users:
  1. Go to your Google account at https://myaccount.google.com/
  2. Go to the Security section.
  3. Enable 2-Step Verification if it's not already enabled.
  4. Visit https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords to create an App Password.
  5. Enter a name for your app (e.g., your name, anything..) and create it.
  6. A modal will appear with the generated password. Copy the password and enter it in the box below.
For Yahoo Users:
  1. Go to Yahoo Account Security.
  2. Enable Two-Step Verification (if not already enabled):
    • Scroll to "Two-step verification" and turn it ON.
    • Add your phone number to receive verification codes.
  3. Generate an App Password:
    • Scroll to "App passwords".
    • Click "Generate app password".
    • Select the app you need (or choose Other App for custom use).
    • Click "Generate" to get a 16-character app password.
